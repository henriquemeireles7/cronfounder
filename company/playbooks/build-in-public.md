# Build in public

Theory of the number: developers follow builders whose problems they share.
Consistent, concrete, honest updates compound into distribution.

## Steps

1. Strategist proposes a bet citing this playbook (`playbook: build-in-public`).
2. Content builder drafts from the real journal — real numbers, real verdicts,
   including the failed bets (honesty is the differentiator).
3. Human gates every push while `autonomy: manual`.

## Autonomy

Earned on record, granted by a human editing the `autonomy:` field above
(the ramp: manual → draft_only → scheduled_with_approval → auto). Verdicts
update `track_record` automatically; promotion stays a human act.
